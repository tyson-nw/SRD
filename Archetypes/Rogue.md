Rogues are always watching for opportunities they can exploit. Their favored [[Stats]] are [[Dexterity]] and [[Wits]]. They are proficient in [[Athletics]], [[Weapons]], [[Social]], [[Stealth]], and two other skills. Rogues have a base [[HP]] of 8. Rogues begin with a Short Sword, a Dagger, and Leather Armor. ^7958f3

#### Tier 1
##### Slippery
As a [[Maneuver]] move twice their [[Speed]], move their [[Speed]] and [[Hide]], or move their [[Speed]] and [[Evade]]. 

##### Sneak Attack 2d6
As an [[Action]] [[Exploit]] a foe to deal weapon damage and deal an additional 2d6. 

#### Tier 2
Rouges gain Sneak Attack and one other Tier 2 [[Features|Feature]].

##### Sneak Attack
As an [[Action]] [[Exploit]] a foe to deal weapon damage and deal an additional 4d6. 

##### Evasion
As a [[Response]] to an area effect the Rogue is within, they can move their speed without provoking a [[Movement Response]]. If a Rogue ends this move outside the area they take no damage.

##### Kneecap
As an [[Action]] [[Exploit]] a foe to deal weapon damage, [[Blighted|Blight]] a physical stat, and the target speed to be halved. 

#### Tier 3
Rouges gain Sneak Attack and two other Tier 2 or 3 [[Features]].

##### Darting Movements
Rogues doesn't provoke a [[Movement Response]].

##### Skill Mastery
Automatically succeed on [[Skills|Skill]] rolls with a [[Moderate]] or lower [[Target Number]].

##### Sneak Attack
As an [[Action]] [[Exploit]] a foe to deal weapon damage and deal an additional 6d6. 

##### Uncanny Dodge
As a Response, a Rogue can replace a failed [[React]] or [[Deflect]] roll with a [[Wits]]+[[Athletics]] roll.

#### Tier 4
Rogues gain Sneak Attack and two other Tier 3 or 4 [[Features]].

##### Elusive
As long as a Rogue is not [[Unconscious]] they cannot have a [[Hindrance]] on a [[Defenses|Defense]] roll, nor can they be [[Expose|Exposed]].

##### Fast Hands
Make an [[Action]] or [[Maneuver]] without the act being seen or making the Rouge no longer [[Hidden]]. 

##### Sneak Attack
As an [[Action]] [[Exploit]] a foe to deal weapon damage and deal an additional 8d6.
