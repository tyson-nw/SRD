## Moonbeam
- **Tier** 1 Wildshaper
- **Casting Time** Action
- **Target** 12 space Range, 3 space Circle, Body
- **Duration** -

A beam shines down dealing 3d6 damage. Half as much on a successful defense. When cast at a higher Tier, add an additional 2d6 for each tier beyond 1st.
