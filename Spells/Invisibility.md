## Invisibility
- **Tier** 2 Wizard
- **Casting Time** Action
- **Target** Touch
- **Duration** 1 minute, Concentration

The target becomes [[Invisible]] to sight until they roll against a creature. When cast at a higher Tier, increase the targets by 2 for every Tier over 2nd.
