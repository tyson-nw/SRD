## Levitate
- **Tier** 1 Wizard
- **Casting Time** Action
- **Target** Self
- **Duration** 1 hour

The caster gains a [[Hover]] speed equal to their movement speed. When cast at a higher Tier, target an additional creature for each Tier beyond 1st.
